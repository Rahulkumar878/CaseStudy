Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my parcel?
You can track your parcel by clicking on the "Track" button in your previous bookings, or by entering your tracking ID on the tracking page.
How do I book a new parcel delivery?
Log in to your account, go to "Book Service" and fill in the required details about your parcel and delivery preferences.
What are the delivery speed options?
We offer three delivery speeds:
  • Same Day Delivery - Delivery within 24 hours
  • Express Delivery - 2-3 business days
  • Standard Delivery - 4-5 business days
How can I get an invoice for my booking?
You can find invoices for completed deliveries in your "Previous Bookings" page. Look for the invoice icon next to delivered parcels.
